About Me

My name is Ashanti Lockhart and I am 18 years old. I was born and raised in Chicago,IL.

It is not easy growing up in the inner city. Many people don't expect much out of minorities.

I am proud to say this year I graduated valedictorian from Chicago Vocational Career Academy.

I am currently enrolled in Richard J Daley community college taking prerequisite to become a nurse.

I love earning money, shopping,pampering myself,and traveling.

I plan to travel the world so I can learn about different cultures and leaving my footprint around the world.
