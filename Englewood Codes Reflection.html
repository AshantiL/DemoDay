What I think about Englewood Codes?

Englewood Codes provides a great opportunity for teens to become interactive in a positive way.

This program allows us to learn more details about technology and how we can become more open minded to it

This program allows peers to know each other since they live in different parts of Chicago to unite as one

How does Englewood Codes impact the community

The instructors not only give us information about current events in technology but around the world also.

We have sessions to expresss our opinions about the session and current events around us

With these sessions it allows us to see our reflection because the instructors wants us to open up more about ourselves so that way we wont feel ashamed.

Englewood Codes is more than just a program. It is like a safe haven
